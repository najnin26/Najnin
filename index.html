Journal ArticleIntelligence-Based Medicine — Elsevier2026
Quantum Computing in Medical Diagnostics and Treatment: A Systematic Review of Trends, Challenges and Future Directions
Najnin Sultana Shirin, Md Mehedi Hasan, Md Kishor Morol, Nafiz Fahad, Md Tanzib Hossain, Md Jakir Hossen, Dip Nandi
This systematic review comprehensively surveys the intersection of quantum computing and medical diagnostics, covering quantum algorithms for imaging analysis, drug discovery, genomic data processing, and treatment optimization. Key trends include variational quantum eigensolvers, quantum kernel methods for disease classification, and hybrid quantum-classical pipelines for clinical decision support.

Conference PaperMECON 2026 — Malaysia2026
ACAM-MobileNetV2: Attention and Contrastive-Aware MobileNetV2 for Multi-Class Viral Skin Lesion Classification
Najnin Sultana Shirin, Shabikun Nahar
We introduce ACAM-MobileNetV2, integrating channel-wise and spatial attention modules with a supervised contrastive loss into MobileNetV2 for multi-class viral skin lesion classification. ACAM-MobileNetV2 outperforms vanilla MobileNetV2 and attention-augmented baselines in macro-F1 and per-class accuracy.

Journal ArticleIEEE Transactions on Quantum Engineering2025
Q-EmotionNet: A Quantum Convolutional Neural Network for Facial Emotion Recognition via Variational Quantum Circuits
Najnin Sultana Shirin, Md Kishor Morol, Tze Hui Liew, Dip Nandi
We propose Q-EmotionNet, a quantum convolutional neural network that encodes facial feature maps into parameterized quantum states and processes them through variational quantum circuits. The model achieves competitive emotion classification accuracy with favorable parameter efficiency compared to classical CNNs.

Journal ArticleQuantum Machine Intelligence — Springer Nature2025
Hybrid Quantum–Classical Transfer Learning Using Vision Transformer Features for Breast Cancer Histopathology
Najnin Sultana Shirin, Md Kishor Morol, Tze Hui Liew, Dip Nandi
A hybrid quantum-classical transfer learning framework for breast cancer histopathology classification. Visual features extracted via a pre-trained Vision Transformer (ViT) are encoded into quantum states and classified through variational quantum circuits. Benchmarked on the BreakHis dataset across multiple magnification levels.

Journal ArticleIEEE Open Access2025
QBERT-LSTM: Quantum Intelligence Based Real-Time Mental Health Detection Using Web Scraping
Najnin Sultana Shirin, Md. Aminul Islam, Maria Akter Abin, Mahmoud Abouyoussef
QBERT-LSTM is a novel quantum-classical hybrid for real-time mental health detection from social media text. Combining quantum-enhanced BERT representations with a classical LSTM model, results demonstrate improvements in precision and recall over classical baselines, particularly for minority-class detection.
